Spinnin' Records

Spinnin' Records is a Dutch electronic music record label founded in 1999 by Eelko van Kooten and Roger de Graaf. In September 2017, Warner Music Group acquired Spinnin' Records for over $100 million. Some artists of Spinnin' Records are Martin Garrix , Jay Hardway , Mike Williams , Tiesto , Afrojack , Don Diablo and Mesto.

Monstercat

Monstercat (formerly known as Monstercat Media) is a Canadian independent electronic music record label based in Vancouver, British Columbia. Founded in 2011 by Mike Darlington and Ari Paunonen, the label released music on a tri-weekly basis until 2018, when this was changed to four releases a week. This would later be changed to six releases a week in 2021. Some artists of Monstercat are, Marshmello, Kaskade , Slushii , Vicetone , Infected Mushroom and Aero Chord.
